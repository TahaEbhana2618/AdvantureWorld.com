specs :-

  • Backyard Skittles crate
  • 12 numbered pins
  • 1 throwing baton
  • Instructions

Simply toss the throwing pin at the numbered pins to earn points. Knocking over a single pin scores you the number of points marked on the pin. If you hit over multiple pins, you score the amount of pins knocked over regardless of their number. The player who reaches exactly 50 points wins the game.
